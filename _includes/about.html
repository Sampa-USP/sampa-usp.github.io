About


The sampa's Quantum Computing group explores the frontier of Quantum Computing as a transformative tool across disciplines, bridging quantum chemistry, materials science, and artificial intelligence. Our work focuses on developing quantum-enhanced computational methods capable of solving problems that are intractable on classical architectures.

We investigate quantum algorithms for electronic structure calculations and catalytic reaction modeling, leveraging both variational quantum eigensolvers, quantum Monte Carlo techniques and Neural Networks to accurately predict ground-state properties and reaction pathways.
